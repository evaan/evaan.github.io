About Me

I'm Evan, a Senior Computer Engineering student at Memorial University of Newfoundland. I am also the Electrical Lead for Eastern Edge Robotics, and a Software Member of Memorial Medtech.

This year, Eastern Edge competed at the MATE ROV World Championship here in St. John's, and Memorial Medtech competed in the True North Biomedical Competition at Western University in London, Ontario. Both teams placed first overall!

In my free time, I work on other projects. One of them is Claret, a schedule builder designed for Memorial University students written in Go and React. I am also working on another project: TASNot, an STM32-based board designed to perform tool-assisted speedruns on real hardware, including the NES and Sega Genesis.
